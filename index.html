Everybody can help make a difference.

An easy way is to donate books in your area. Stores such as Goodwill and the Salvation Army take book donations. Websites like BookSmiles allow contributions, and you can register to get books from them!

Looking Locally

Another great way to help is to volunteer at literacy centers! Many middle and elementary schools offer after-school literacy clubs. These programs give you a chance to give back to your community and help kids with their language skills.

 For more information, search "literacy programs" in your area. Some come directly through schools or 3rd party sources like libraries and colleges.

A Global Look

Wanting to help donate books and textbooks? Many websites allow you to donate books and ebooks to other countries. Websites like the International Book Project and Books for Africa permit you to help.
